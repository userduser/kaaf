Privacy and Donation Policy

Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Kaaf (Knowledge and Action Foundation), we are committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you engage with our services, website, or donate to our charity.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you donate, sign up for newsletters, or participate in our programs, we may collect personal details such as your name, address, email, phone number, and payment information.
  • Non-Personal Information: We may also gather non-personal data such as browser type, IP address, and usage patterns for analytics and website improvements.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process donations and provide receipts
  • Communicate updates, newsletters, and program information
  • Enhance user experience on our website
  • Meet legal obligations and requirements

Data Protection

We implement security measures to ensure the protection of your personal information against unauthorized access or disclosure. Your data will never be sold or shared with third parties for commercial purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections or deletions of your data
  • Opt out of receiving communication from us at any time

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies to personalize your experience and for analytical purposes. You can manage cookie settings through your browser.

Donation Policy

Introduction

Kaaf (Knowledge and Action Foundation) relies on generous contributions to fund our charitable programs. This donation policy outlines our procedures for handling donations, refunds, and related matters.

Donation Types

We accept the following types of donations:

  • One-Time Donations: Contributions made via our website or through other authorized channels.
  • Recurring Donations: Monthly or periodic donations set up through our donation platform.
  • In-Kind Donations: Goods or services provided to support our work.

Use of Donations

Donations are utilized to support our various charitable initiatives, including but not limited to:

  • Education programs
  • Food and medical aid
  • Orphan and widow support
  • Emergency relief efforts

Refund Policy

Donations are generally non-refundable. However, if there has been an error or duplication in your donation, please contact us within 14 days, and we will assess the request on a case-by-case basis.

Transparency

Kaaf is committed to transparency. We regularly provide updates and reports on how donations are being used and the impact they have made. We also ensure that all donations are processed securely.

For any questions or concerns regarding our Privacy or Donation policies, please contact us at Info@EgyptAid.org .

KAAF is a charitable foundation taken on by students studying in Egypt who were moved the poverty in the surrounding areas.
